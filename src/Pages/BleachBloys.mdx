# Bleach Bloys

[bleachbloys.bandcamp.com](https://bleachbloys.bandcamp.com)

I started a project with some close friends in 2020
called Bleach Bloys. The goal of it is to improvise
and to take that improvisation seriously. Nothing we
put out has ever been rehearsed, it's all created in
the moment, for the moment. Bleach Bloys records are
more like a souvenir from the jam session than they
are a complete piece.
